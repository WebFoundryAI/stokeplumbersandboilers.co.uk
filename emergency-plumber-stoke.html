What to Do in a Plumbing Emergency

  1. Turn off the water - Locate your stopcock (usually under the kitchen sink) and turn it clockwise to shut off the water supply
  2. If gas related - Turn off the gas at the meter and open windows. If you smell gas strongly, leave the property
  3. Call us immediately - Phone 01782 123456 and describe the situation
  4. Contain the damage - Use towels and containers to collect water while waiting for our arrival
  5. Turn off electrics if necessary - If water is near electrical fittings, switch off at the consumer unit
