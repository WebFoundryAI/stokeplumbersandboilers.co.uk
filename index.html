Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency plumbing in Stoke-on-Trent?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services across Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire. Our emergency team handles burst pipes, severe leaks, blocked drains, and boiler breakdowns at any hour. Call 01782 123456 for immediate assistance.

Are your engineers Gas Safe registered?

Absolutely. All our gas engineers are Gas Safe registered and carry their identification cards at all times. They are fully qualified to work on boilers, central heating systems, gas fires, and gas cookers. You can verify their registration on the Gas Safe Register website.

What boiler brands do you service?

We service and repair all major boiler brands including Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal, Viessmann, Glow-worm, Potterton, and more. Our engineers carry common spare parts and have access to manufacturer support for complex repairs.

Do you provide boiler installations?

Yes, we supply and install new boilers from leading manufacturers. Our heating surveys ensure you get the right boiler for your property, and our competitive pricing includes full installation, system flushing, and disposal of your old boiler.
