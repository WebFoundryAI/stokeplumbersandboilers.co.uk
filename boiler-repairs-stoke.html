Professional Boiler Repair Services

When your boiler stops working, you need a fast, reliable repair service. Stoke Plumbers and Boilers provides expert boiler repairs across Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire, carried out by Gas Safe registered engineers with extensive experience across all major boiler brands.

We diagnose faults accurately and carry out repairs efficiently, using quality replacement parts that meet manufacturer specifications. Our aim is always to restore your heating and hot water as quickly as possible whilst ensuring the repair is lasting and safe.

Common Boiler Problems We Fix

  • No heating or hot water - Multiple possible causes diagnosed and repaired
  • Boiler not igniting - Ignition electrode, gas valve, and PCB issues
  • Low boiler pressure - Leak detection and pressure vessel repairs
  • Boiler making noises - Kettling, banging, and pump noise resolution
  • Error codes displaying - Fault code diagnosis and component replacement
  • Pilot light problems - Thermocouple and gas supply issues
  • Radiators not heating - System balancing and motorised valve repairs
  • Boiler leaking - Heat exchanger, pump seal, and valve leaks

Boiler Servicing

Annual boiler servicing is essential for safety, efficiency, and maintaining your warranty. Our comprehensive service includes gas safety checks, combustion analysis, component inspection, and cleaning. We recommend servicing before winter to ensure reliable operation when you need it most.

Need a new boiler? We also offer boiler installations and central heating services with competitive pricing and extended warranties.
