Plumbing and Heating Services

Stoke Plumbers and Boilers provides comprehensive plumbing and heating services across Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire. Our Gas Safe registered engineers and experienced plumbers deliver quality solutions for every requirement.

  • Emergency Plumber

    24/7 emergency response for burst pipes, severe leaks, blocked drains, and boiler breakdowns. Fast arrival times across Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire.

  • Boiler Repairs

    Expert diagnosis and repair of all boiler makes and models by Gas Safe registered engineers. We also provide annual boiler servicing.

  • Central Heating

    New boiler installations, system upgrades, radiator fitting, power flushing, and complete heating system design and installation.

  • Bathroom Installations

    Complete bathroom fitting from design to completion. En-suites, wet rooms, and full bathroom renovations professionally installed.
