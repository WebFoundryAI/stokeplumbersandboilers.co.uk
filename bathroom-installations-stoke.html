Bathroom Installations in Stoke-on-Trent

Complete bathroom design and fitting services

Professional Bathroom Fitting Services

Transform your bathroom with Stoke Plumbers and Boilers. We provide complete bathroom installation services across Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire, managing every aspect from initial design through to the finishing touches. Our experienced team ensures your new bathroom is beautiful, functional, and built to last.

Whether you are planning a full bathroom renovation, adding an en-suite, or converting to a wet room, we deliver results that exceed expectations. We work with your chosen fixtures or can recommend quality products from trusted suppliers at competitive prices.

Our Bathroom Services

  • Complete bathroom installations - Full strip-out and refitting
  • En-suite bathrooms - Creating additional facilities
  • Wet room conversions - Accessible shower rooms
  • Shower installations - Electric, mixer, and digital showers
  • Bath replacements - Standard, freestanding, and corner baths
  • Sanitary ware fitting - Toilets, basins, and bidets
  • Bathroom repairs - Leaks, replacements, and upgrades

What We Include

Our bathroom installation service covers all trades required for a complete transformation:

  • All plumbing work including waste and supply pipes
  • Removal and disposal of existing bathroom
  • Floor and wall preparation
  • Coordination of tiling and electrical work
  • Installation of all sanitary ware and fixtures
  • Silicone sealing and finishing
  • Final testing and handover

Design Consultation

Not sure where to start? Our free design consultation helps you plan your perfect bathroom. We discuss your requirements, measure the space, identify any constraints, and suggest layouts that maximise functionality. We can recommend products that suit your style and budget.

Quality Workmanship

Every bathroom we install is completed to the highest standards. Our plumbers are skilled in working with all types of fixtures from leading brands. We take pride in neat pipework, secure fittings, and watertight seals that prevent problems for years to come.

Areas Covered

We install bathrooms throughout Stoke-on-Trent including Hanley, Longton, Stoke, and all surrounding areas. Our service extends to Newcastle-under-Lyme, Stafford, and across Staffordshire.
